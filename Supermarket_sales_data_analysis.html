Supermarket Sales Analysis

This project analyzes supermarket sales data to identify customer purchasing patterns and key factors affecting revenue.

Main objectives:

  • Analyze sales performance;
  • Identify best-selling product categories;
  • Compare revenue across branches;
  • Examine customer behavior;
  • Provide business insights and recommendations.
