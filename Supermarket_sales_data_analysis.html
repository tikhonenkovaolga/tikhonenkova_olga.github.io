Supermarket Sales Analysis

This project analyzes supermarket sales data to understand customer behavior, product performance, and revenue trends.

Project Goals

  • Analyze sales by product categories
  • Identify peak sales periods
  • Find top-performing products and branches
  • Explore relationship between price and revenue

Tools Used

Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Key Insights

Sales vary significantly across product categories and time periods. Certain branches consistently outperform others in revenue generation.
