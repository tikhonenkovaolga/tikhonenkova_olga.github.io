Perfume Sales Analysis

This project analyzes the online perfume market and investigates the factors influencing the sales performance of fragrance products.

Main objectives:

  • Analyze the online perfume market;
  • Identify top-selling fragrance brands;
  • Compare sales volume and revenue;
  • Evaluate the impact of price on sales performance;
  • Determine the key factors affecting perfume sales and revenue.
