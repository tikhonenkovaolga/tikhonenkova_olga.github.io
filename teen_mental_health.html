Teen Mental Health and Social Media Analysis

This project investigates the relationship between social media usage and mental health among teenagers. The analysis aims to identify patterns and factors associated with anxiety, depression, and overall well-being.

Main objectives:

  • Examine social media usage patterns among teenagers;
  • Analyze the relationship between screen time and mental health indicators;
  • Identify factors associated with anxiety and depression;
  • Compare mental health outcomes across different demographic groups;
  • Provide insights that may help support adolescent well-being.
