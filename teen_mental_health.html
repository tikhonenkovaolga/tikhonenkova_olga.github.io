Teen Mental Health Analysis

This project analyzes factors affecting adolescent mental health using data analysis and visualization techniques.

The study examines relationships between age, gender, academic pressure, lifestyle habits, and indicators of mental well-being.

Python libraries such as Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn were used to explore the data and identify key patterns and trends.

The findings provide valuable insights into the challenges faced by teenagers and highlight the importance of mental health awareness and support.
